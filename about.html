About Us

Welcome to Hotel Vistara Grand

Welcome to Hotel Vistara Grand, your gateway to serenity in the heart of Haridwar.

Nestled along the sacred banks of the Ganges River, our hotel offers an oasis of tranquility amidst the vibrant and culturally rich city of Haridwar. At Hotel Vistara Grand, we take pride in providing you with a warm and welcoming home away from home.

Our story began with a deep-rooted love for the timeless charm and spirituality of Haridwar. It was our dream to create a haven where guests could experience the beauty, culture, and spirituality of this holy city while enjoying the modern comforts and conveniences of a world-class hotel.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be enveloped in an atmosphere of elegance and hospitality. Our well-appointed rooms and suites offer the perfect blend of contemporary design and traditional Indian aesthetics, ensuring a comfortable and peaceful stay. Whether you're a pilgrim seeking spiritual solace, a traveler exploring the city's rich history, or a business professional in need of a restful retreat, Hotel Vistara Grand has the ideal accommodation for you.

Hotel Vistara Grand is also equipped with modern amenities to cater to the needs of business travelers, making it an excellent choice for corporate events, conferences, and meetings. We have state-of-the-art facilities to ensure your events are a success.

Haridwar's spiritual and cultural treasures are at your doorstep when you stay with us. The famous Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri, the mesmerizing beauty of Chandi Devi and Mansa Devi temples, and the historic Rajaji National Park are just a few of the attractions that await you.

At Hotel Vistara Grand, we are not just in the business of offering accommodation; we are committed to creating memorable experiences for our guests. We invite you to join us on your journey through Haridwar and create lasting memories in the lap of tranquility.

Thank you for choosing Hotel Vistara Grand, and we look forward to being a part of your Haridwar adventure. Your story begins here.
